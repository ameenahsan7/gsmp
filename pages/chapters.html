For Chapter Leads

How Chapters Work

1

Apply

Fill out a short application telling us about your institution and why you want to lead a chapter. We review applications within 48 hours.

2

Get Your Starter Kit

Approved leads receive the GSMP Chapter Starter Kit: branding assets, event templates, leadership guidelines, and a dedicated Discord channel.

3

Launch & Grow

Recruit members at your school, host your first event, and build your local GSMP community. We support you with resources, mentorship, and network-wide visibility.
